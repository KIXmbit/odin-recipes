Deviled Egg Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 pound potatoes (for 2 cups diced cooked potatoes)
  • 4 large hard boiled eggs, mashed
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons minced onions
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 stalk celery, diced

Steps

  • Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Drain. When potatoes are cool enough to handle, peel and cut into 1/2-inch cubes.
  • Add potatoes, eggs, mayo, mustard, onion and celery to a bowl and stir until well combined. Chill at least 1 hour prior to serving.
